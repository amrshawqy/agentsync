# Code of conduct

This project follows the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1**.

You can read the full text here:
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

## In short

We want AgentSync to be a welcoming and productive place to collaborate, regardless of background or experience level. Be kind, assume good faith, and focus on the work.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness a violation, please contact the maintainers privately. See [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) for the contact channel — for sensitive personal reports, prefer the same private channel rather than a public issue.

Maintainers will investigate every report and respond as promptly as we can. We will keep your identity confidential to the extent allowed by the situation.
